Memoryhouse Magazine, Editor in Chief / Chief Design Editor (2018-2020) I was responsible for the curation and layout of a semi-annual print and digital literary magazine that accepts submissions globally around a set theme. During my tenure, I pioneered the integration of our visual submissions (artwork, photographs) with our literary content (poems, prose) as well as the increased fluidity of titles and other auxiliary content placement to enhance interpretation of the pieces.

These new templates and designs have been passed down and re-used every issue since. Unattributed images are the work of our creative team, which I also oversaw.

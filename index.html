Design

"In words and pickles, I have immortalized my memories, although distortions are inevitable in both methods." - Salman Rushdie

I celebrate pickles, from the tart sour fluid pinching the tip of my tongue to the tingly sweetness that lingers in my throat. In my music, design and writing, I aim to package totalities without tediousness, to immortalize that which is important but often overlooked. My training in economics and psychology have instilled a near-obsessive need to understand the strategic value and the full implications of the content I generate. Thus, my forte is poignancy - wrapping power in subtlety, artful references into discreet canvases, making your audience think/look/read/listen twice, like a chocolate ganache with an exigent hint of orange zest.

The world and increasingly, data, is pickle juice - a myriad of flavors and grains, not all of them appealing, battling to engrave themselves onto just a shred of cucumber flesh. Give me your pickle juice, and let us make pickles!
